# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members of the AI Agent Superpowers community, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Assuming good intent when interpreting messages

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Insults or derogatory comments
- Spam or commercial solicitation
- Any conduct that violates laws or regulations

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community spaces (GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests, email)
- Official project spaces (repository, documentation)
- Public spaces where someone represents the project

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to:
- **GitHub Issue**: File a private security report if available
- **Email**: Contact the project maintainer
- **GitHub Discussions**: Flag for moderator review

Please include:
- Description of the incident
- Links to relevant messages (if applicable)
- What impact it had
- Any additional context

### Investigation & Response

- All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially
- The maintainer will investigate and determine appropriate action
- Actions may include warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent removal
- The reporter will be informed of the outcome (to the extent appropriate)
